About the firm

A private trading and supply house.

Noah 1123 is a principal trading and supply house. The firm sources, contracts, and ships across three lines — healthcare consumables for institutional procurement, American classic and collector automobiles into the Gulf, and project-based trade brokerage for engagements that fall outside standard retail channels.

The firm operates principal-to-principal. Engagements are conducted under formal terms, with confirmed purchase orders, back-to-back credit lines where the category requires, and bonded shipping where regulation requires. Discretion is the default posture. The firm does not publish client lists.
